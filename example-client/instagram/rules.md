# @the.ethanlim — Instagram Rules

Hard rules every IG deliverable must follow.

## Voice rules
- Personal-brand reels: empathetic, vulnerable, real. Never performative. Never "guru."
- Business reels: direct, no-BS, conversational. Lead with proof, not hype.
- No em dashes anywhere. Use commas, periods, or restructure.
- No emojis.
- Contractions throughout. No corporate fluff.

## Reel format rules
- First draft = teleprompter only (spoken words). Production doc only after approval.
- Match an archetype from `context/sops/viral-content/reel-archetypes.md`. Don't generate generic templates.
- Real numbers with cents/decimals. Named students. Identity hooks.
- Reference priority for personal brand: Mino → Soo → Nik → Hormozi → Gadzhi.

## Hard "do not" list
- Don't reference NS in any deliverable (Ethan didn't serve due to scheduling — check with Ethan if any military-style framing is needed).
- Don't invent statistics, dollar amounts, or student names.
- Don't post content that conflates the personal brand and the Easyinfo business audience.
- Don't use AI-slop patterns (em dashes, "Here's the thing," fragment chains, contrast reframes, "delve," "leverage").

Add new rules here as they get validated through real posts and feedback.
