# Content Pillars: Ethan / Easyinfo

## Business Content Pillars (Easyinfo YouTube + IG)

### Pillar 1: The Infrastructure Gap
Content that exposes why coaches plateau at $30-50K/month. The problem isn't their marketing, their offer, or their traffic. It's their backend. No systems, no SOPs, no CRM, no team infrastructure. Show the gap between what they're doing (front-end hustle) and what they're missing (backend operations).

### Pillar 2: The 7-Phase Breakdown
Educational content walking through each phase of the EasyScale System. What it includes, why it matters, what it looks like when it's built vs. when it's missing. Position Easyinfo as the only partner that covers all 7 under one roof.

### Pillar 3: Results and Case Studies
Specific client outcomes. Before/after. Revenue numbers. Time reclaimed. Systems built. Always with specifics, never vague. "Jorge was stuck at $28K/month. Eight months later, $180K/month."

### Pillar 4: Corruption Narratives
Content that calls out why every other "solution" fails. Why coaches teach but don't build. Why agencies handle one silo. Why VAs need more hand-holding than they save. Why fractional COOs advise but don't implement. Position EasyScale as the only model that actually solves the problem.

### Pillar 5: The Business Owner's Trap
Emotional content about the burnout-success paradox. Publicly successful, privately drowning. Built a job, not a business. Started for freedom, now more trapped than when they had a 9-to-5. This bridges business and personal brand.

## Personal Brand Content Pillars (@the.ethanlim YouTube + IG)

### Pillar 1: Building in Public
Real-time documentation of building Easyinfo while studying for A-Levels. Revenue updates, wins, failures, daily grind. Not polished. Not curated. Real.

### Pillar 2: The Conventional Path Problem
Content about why the PSLE > O-Levels > JC > NS > Degree > Job > BTO pipeline isn't the only option. Not anti-education, but pro-choice. Show that there's another way and you're living proof.

### Pillar 3: The Self-Improvement Trap
Content that calls out the consumption loop. Watching Hormozi and Iman Gadzhi for 2 years and having nothing to show for it. Analysis paralysis. Starting and quitting. The gap between knowing what to do and actually doing it.

### Pillar 4: Emotional Connection
Vulnerability content. Loneliness on the entrepreneurial path. Parental pressure. Imposter syndrome. Moments of doubt. The stuff nobody talks about. Make the audience feel seen and not alone.

### Pillar 5: Practical How-To
Tactical content on what actually works. How to start an online business. How to get your first client. How to manage school and business. Specific, actionable, based on what Ethan actually did.

---

## Notes

- Business pillars 1-4 map directly to the Easyinfo funnel. They educate, build authority, and pre-sell the EasyScale System.
- Personal brand pillars 1-4 build trust, community, and long-term audience. Pillar 5 converts followers into leads for Easyinfo over time.
- Some content crosses both audiences (Pillar 5 on business side, Pillar 1 on personal side). Use both voices as appropriate.
